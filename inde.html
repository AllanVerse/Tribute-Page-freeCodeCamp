Satoru Gojo

I'm The Strongest

What is Satoru Gojo known for?

Gojo is known for his superhuman levels of cursed energy. He also has a powerful cursed energy technique called Six Eyes, which is an innate ability passed down through the Gojo bloodline. This technique gives Gojo nearly supreme control over his cursed energy.

Why Gojo Is Powerful ?

Not only does Gojo possess multiple rare talents, but he also has a massive amount of cursed energy. Coupled with tremendous physical strength, intelligence and agility, it is no wonder why he is the strongest jujutsu sorcerer.
